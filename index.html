Days Gone Runs at 60 Frames-Per-Second, Dynamic 4K on PS5

Days Gone will take advantage of Sony’s next-gen hardware at launch, running at “up to 60 frames-per-second in dynamic 4K” on the PlayStation 5. The title was previously locked at 30 frames-per-second on the PS4 Pro, and utilised a checkerboard rendering solution to resolve a 4K image. What does this mean in layman’s terms? The Oregon-based apocalyptic open worlder will both look and play better on the PS5. It’s not the first game to be specifically updated for the Japanese giant’s fresh format: Ghost of Tsushima will also run at 60 frames-per-second. There’s never been a better time to enjoy Deacon St. John’s debut, especially seeing as it’s included with the PS Plus Collection at launch. We liked the title a lot in our review, describing it as “gaming comfort food”. Will you be saddling up all over again?
